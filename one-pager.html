Frequently Asked Questions & Details
Common Questions

How is this different from Hootsuite or Buffer?

Those are tools — you still figure out what to post, write it, and schedule it. Upriff is a service. We handle strategy, creation, and publishing for you.

How is this different from an agency?

Agencies are great — and when you're ready, we have that path too. Upriff gives you the core elements of what an agency delivers — strategy, content, publishing — at a price that makes sense for where your business is right now.

Will it actually sound like my business?

Yes. We capture your brand voice — your tone, language, and personality — during onboarding. Every piece of content is built from that foundation.

I've tried AI before and it didn't stick.

That's exactly the point. You tried using AI as a tool. With Upriff, we use the AI — it's a service that runs behind the scenes so you don't have to.

Can I review content before it posts?

Absolutely. You can review and approve content, request changes, or let it run hands-off. Upriff works either way.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Go month-to-month starting at $649/mo, or commit for 6 months at a time starting at $499/mo and save.
