Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Profit Now Solutions, LLC, doing business as Muldrow Marketing ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you visit upriff.io ("Website") or use the Upriff managed social media marketing service ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Consultation form submissions: Name, email address, phone number, business name, website, and a description of your marketing needs
  • Onboarding intake: Business details, brand voice preferences, social media account information, target audience description, and content preferences
  • Account and billing: Payment information is collected and processed by Stripe, our payment processor. We do not store your credit card details on our servers

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Website usage: upriff.io is a static website. We do not use analytics tracking, advertising pixels, or third-party cookies by default. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated
  • Social media performance data: If you subscribe to the Service, we collect engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, impressions) from your connected social media accounts to track content performance and improve strategy

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Website and Service:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe collects and processes your payment information under their own Privacy Policy
  • GoHighLevel — CRM, form submissions, email communication, and calendar booking. Information you submit through our forms is stored in GoHighLevel
  • Cloudflare — Website hosting and delivery. Cloudflare may collect minimal technical data (IP addresses, request headers) as part of serving the Website
  • Social media platforms — When you connect your accounts (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Google Business Profile, X/Twitter), we access them through their official APIs to publish content and retrieve performance data

5. Cookies

upriff.io is a static website and does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services (such as embedded forms) may set their own cookies. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

Active clients: We retain your business information, brand voice profile, content history, and performance data for the duration of your subscription and for 90 days after cancellation to facilitate potential re-activation.

Consultation leads: If you submit a consultation form but do not become a client, we retain your contact information for up to 12 months for follow-up purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

Billing records: Payment and billing records are retained as required by applicable tax and accounting laws.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all website and service interactions
  • Access controls limiting who within our organization can view client data
  • Secure third-party processors (Stripe for payments, Cloudflare for hosting)

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@upriff.io.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or by posting a notice on our Website. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.
